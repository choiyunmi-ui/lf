MOGG is a high-end brand for women who want chic and trendy fashion with a comfortable design and style. The brand offers a variety of items to allow people to arrange their own style combination, such as the minimal yet fashionable French chic style. Since the launch of the brand in 2006, items, including long jackets, tailored collar coats, rider jackets, and baggy pants, have become steady sellers. MOGG maintains its design philosophy and enhances its luxurious brand image through the best European fabrics and top models for ad campaigns.
