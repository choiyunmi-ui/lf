CEO Greetings

We commit to becoming a great and valuable company that you will be proud of.

LF, Life in Future, promotes a future-oriented lifestyle brand that gives dreams and touching moments to customers by creating a tailored style of living for each individual with new perspective.
Fashion is more than just clothes. Fashion reflects the cultural trend of the time as well as one’s lifestyle and character. It represents the philosophy of designers and brands. In other words, fashion refers to all sectors that show the emotions and feelings of people. We have endeavored to highlight your life through our fashion brands on special and memorable occasions, such as first day at school, first day at work, birthdays, weddings, and business meetings.

Currently, we lead the Korean fashion industry with passion as a total fashion company with lines that range from menswear, women’s wear, casual wear, sportswear, outdoor wear, and accessories, to select shops. Established since 1974, we have set new trends over the past 40 years that suggest unique and different styles to enrich the lives of customers. In particular, some of our brands are recognized as the leading brands of our time, such as MAESTRO (menswear with innovative pattern), DAKS (British traditional look), Hazzys (modern traditional casual look), and Lafuma (European outdoor wear). The great achievements of our brands are the results of our persistent research, proactive investment, resource development, and capacity enhancement in design and marketing. Furthermore, we also focus on our creative corporate culture that helps to build leadership and our sensible services that put customers before us.

As the best fashion culture company in Korea, we seek to become one of the top lifestyle companies in the world. We will strengthen our internal capacities, such as design, quality, resources, and corporate culture, to achieve our goal. In addition, we will strive to develop some of our key brands as global high-end brands while laying the foundation for sustainable growth by proactively penetrating overseas markets and pioneering new projects.

We strive to turn LF Mall into a global lifestyle company to enrich the lifestyles of our customers in every way possible. We commit to becoming a great and valuable company that you will be proud of. We will open the door to the future with strong passion and outstanding creativity and ready for endless challenges.

President & CEO KOO Bon Keul
