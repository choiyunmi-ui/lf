HISTORY

LF, established in 1953, has changed the dynamics of the Korean fashion clothing industry, stood at the forefront in advancing Korean fashion industry through sustainable infrastructure investment, and created the image of a “fashion culture company” by promoting various cultural projects.

  • Develop as a global company | 2014 ~ 2006
  • Lead the advancement of the fashion industry | 2005 ~ 1953
We will develop as a global company
As a fashion house that has a competitive edge in the global market, we will further strengthen our brand values and core capacities to prioritize customer needs. We will become a lifestyle company that provides a tailored style of living to customers rather than confining them to fashion.
