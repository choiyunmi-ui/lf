HISTORY

LF, established in 1953, has changed the dynamics of the Korean fashion clothing industry, stood at the forefront in advancing Korean fashion industry through sustainable infrastructure investment, and created the image of a “fashion culture company” by promoting various cultural projects.

  • Develop as a global company | 2014 ~ 2006
  • Lead the advancement of the fashion industry | 2005 ~ 1953
We stood at the forefront in advancing the fashion industry
The history of LF dates back to the establishment and development of Lak Hui Industry and Bando Company in the 1950s. We have strengthened our competitive edge through sustainable innovation management, stood at the forefront in advancing Korean fashion industry through sustainable infrastructure investment, and created the image of a “fashion culture company” by promoting various cultural projects.
